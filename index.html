1. Source of Information

The App aggregates job notifications from official government recruitment portals including central, state, and district-level websites.

  • UPSC – https://upsc.gov.in
  • SSC – https://ssc.gov.in
  • IBPS – https://ibps.in
  • Railways – https://indianrailways.gov.in
  • Employment News – https://employmentnews.gov.in
  • National Career Service – https://ncs.gov.in
  • Various PSC, district, and department portals (.gov.in / .nic.in)

The app may reference 200+ official government websites across India. Due to volume, not all sources are listed here.

Users must verify all information from the official website linked in each job post.

The app does not allow users to apply for jobs directly.
